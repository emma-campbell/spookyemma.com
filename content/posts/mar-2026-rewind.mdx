# Notable

**Hiking** - It's been unseasonably warm here in Indiana this March, so there has been tons of hiking and walking with Moose. We found this hidden gem near us called Leonard Springs, and it was a short and sweet hiking trail on the remains of the old reservoir here in Bloomington.


![Quick snap of the springs @ Leonard Springs Park](/images/posts/mar-2026-rewind/IMG_3689.jpeg)

**Project Hail Mary** - Indiana always manages to surprise me in some of the most unexpected ways. Strangely enough, one of thirteen theaters in the country that shows 70mm iMax lives in the Indiana State Museum, so we ventured up to Indianapolis to see the movie. It was 👎

![Commemorative film strip given out after the 70mm viewing](/images/posts/mar-2026-rewind/IMG_3744.jpeg)

After the viewing, they gave out commemorative film strips for the occasion. I think Andrea and I will definitely attempt to get back there for showings of the Odyssey and Dune 3 if we can.

**A New Bike?** - I'm fighting the urge to buy a gravel bike. There are tons of gravel roads here in south central indiana, and sometimes I need to motivate myself to leave the house. Part of me also wonders if Moose would find bikejoring enjoyable. 

Someone, talk me out of it 😬
