# Notable

**PC Build** - I built my first PC which I did a quick parts list of [here](https://spookyemma.com/notebook/my-pc-build). It was fun! It had been a long time since I had messed with anything tech wise like that, and I had a blast actually (physically) putting something together

**The Newfields Museum** -  Andrea and I went to the [Newfields Museum of Art](https://discovernewfields.org/) for Valentine's Day. If you live within a reasonable distance of Indianapolis, I would highly recommend planning a visit. It's larger than one would expect, taking up 4 floors of large exhibits. During our visit, we got to the [Connection; Land, Water, and Sky. Art from Indigenous Australians](https://www.indianapolismonthly.com/arts-and-culture/newfields-new-lume-exhibit/) before it left. Andrea really loved [Resplendent Dreams: Reawakening the Rococo](https://discovernewfields.org/events-exhibitions/resplendent-dreams-reawakening-the-rococo), which featured works from Diego Montoya who we had previous seen in [¡Presente! about from the National Museum of the American Latino](https://latino.si.edu/exhibitions/presente). Presente was one of the last exhibits we went to before moving from D.C to Indiana, so it was nice to draw some connections.
